ESTOFEX products are issued to assist other meteorologists in forecasting severe weather. National meteorological services issue official warnings for your region. Those warnings can be accessed via their respective websites and on meteoalarm.eu.

ESTOFEX deliberately offers its services without any funding or advertisement. Over 2014 and 2015, donations from 60 visitors helped us cover 95% of the web server costs.
